 THE STORY

The origin of Marvel Studios

Marvel Studios, LLC(originally known as Marvel Films from 1993–1996) is an American film studio based on The Walt Disney Studios in Burbank, California. Formerly a division of Marvel Entertainment, the studio is a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Studios, which is a wholly owned division of The Walt Disney Company, with film producer Kevin Feige serving as chairman. Since 2008, Marvel Studios has released 30 movies in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, from Iron Man (2008) to Black Panther: Eternal Wakanda (2022), 8 TV series since 2021, from WandaVision (2021) to She-Hulk: Attorney at Law (2022) and two TV specials, Werewolf by Night (2022) and The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special (2022). Drama What If...? (2021) is the studio's first animated product. These movies and TV series all have continuity with each other, along with studio-produced One-Shots. The TV series produced by Marvel Television also acknowledges continuity.
